About

We offer exceptional service with complimentary hugs.



Log 9 Materials Scientific Private Limited is a graphene research and development company. At Log 9 Materials we are determined to accelerate the commercialization of graphene nanotechnology. We have just developed the first commercial product on graphene- 'PPuF'. This innovative graphene based cigarette smoke filtration technology reduces the toxins in smoke by up to 50%. We offer a wide range of high quality graphene (CVD graphene, rGO) and its derivatives. We bring to you graphene as researchers and not just as manufactures, so we understand the importance of the grade of our products.



Why Choose Us

We love what we do, and we bring excellence and innovation to each product we work on. We are determined to provide our customers with the purest quality graphene and derivatives having the best chemical, optical, electrical and mechanical properties. We bring to you nano-materials as researchers and not just as manufactures, so we understand the importance of the grade of our products.

Vision

To bridge the gap between graphene technology at labscale to amazing products with our focus on innovation and design. Log 9 Materials is not just a graphene manufacturer, but also a technology and design company with a focus on graphene based product innovation.
